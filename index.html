Frequently Asked Questions

How much does waste clearance cost?

Prices depend on waste type, volume, weight, and access. Share photos and postcode for a firm quote.

What can’t you take?

We can’t collect hazardous chemicals, asbestos, gas cylinders, or medical waste without specialist arrangements.

Do you offer same-day clearance?

Yes, same-day slots are often available depending on demand and your location.

What if access is difficult?

No problem—let us know about stairs, narrow paths, or parking limits when requesting your quote.

Can you remove heavy soil and rubble?

Yes. Heavy loads are priced separately due to disposal weight charges.

Is this better than hiring a skip?

Often yes for full or mixed clearances. No permits, no waiting, and we do the loading for you.

How do I pay?

Most customers pay by bank transfer or card on completion. Business invoicing available.

What is your minimum job size?

Minimum job is half-load (or minimum charge applies).
